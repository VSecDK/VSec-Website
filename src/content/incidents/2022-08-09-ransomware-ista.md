## Description

All IT systems at Ista Danmark A/S were immediately shut down. Systems were offline for over 3 weeks. Daixin Team claimed to have encrypted thousands of servers and later published stolen company data.

## Timeline

- 2022-07-25: Systems taken offline to contain the attack
- 2022-07-29: Ista posts on Twitter about the outage
- 2022-08-09: Danish media picks up the story
- 2022-08-10: Berlingske/Version2 — entering third week of outage
- 2022-08-16: Daixin Team publishes stolen company data
