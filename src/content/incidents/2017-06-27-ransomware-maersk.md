## Description

The NotPetya ransomware attack hit Mærsk and caused a complete shutdown of container terminals globally. The company was forced to rebuild its entire infrastructure from scratch. The attack cost the company billions of DKK and is considered one of the most destructive cyberattacks in history.

The attack is attributed to a state-sponsored actor. NotPetya spread via a trojanized update to the M.E.Doc accounting software.
