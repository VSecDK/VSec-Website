## Description

Lokaltog A/S (regional Danish railways) was hit by Black Basta ransomware. The company was reportedly also hit by ransomware the previous year (2021).

## Timeline

- 2022-07-07: Red Packet Security reports the attack publicly
- 2022-07-13: Version2 publishes story — "Sjællandske lokalbaner hacket af nyt digitalt kartel"
- 2022-07-18: Lokaltog speaks out; confirms also hit by ransomware in 2021
