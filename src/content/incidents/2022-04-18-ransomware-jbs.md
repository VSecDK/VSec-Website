## Description

JBS Textile Group — one of Denmark's largest manufacturers of underwear — was hit by a double extortion attack. Data was exfiltrated and leaked publicly, including personal information on the CEO and top management.

## Timeline

- 2022-04-18: Ransomware attack occurred
- 2022-06-27: Data published on Black Basta leak site
