## Description

A crippling cyberattack took down Würth Danmark's phone, email, and ordering systems. Company policy: no communication with criminals — rebuild only. Estimated loss: ~1,000,000 DKK in revenue between Christmas and New Year's Eve. German IT specialists were brought in to assist with recovery.

## Timeline

- 2021-12-22: Hit by cyberattack — phone, email and ordering systems taken down
- 2021-12-28: Ordering system back online (6 days after the attack)
- 2022 (early): Phone and email systems fully restored
