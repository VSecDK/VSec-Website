## Description

Ransomware hit 7-Eleven Denmark on the morning of 8 August 2022 and forced all 176 Danish stores to close. Ransom demand: 1,000,000 XMR (Monero). 7-Eleven refused to pay. Basic store operations recovered relatively quickly after the attack.

## Timeline

- 2022-08-08: Ransomware hits — all 176 stores forced to close
- 2022-08-09: 5 stores reopen; ransom demand reported by Nordjyske (~$1M USD)
- 2022-08-10: 7-Eleven publicly confirms ransomware attack and ransom demand
- 2022-08-11: 7-Eleven publicly confirms refusal to pay
- 2022-08-26: Still partially affected — recovery ongoing
